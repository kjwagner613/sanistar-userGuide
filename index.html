Getting Started

  • Greg Kempker is your local expert, also we are always available to assist.
  • Any system is only as good as the data it is fed. Completing full customer records, logging interactions and working with the workflow will facilitate successful and efficient transition from initial order to shipping.
  • Should the data ever drift from actual, there are corrective measures to re-align.
  • When you go live, it is important from that point forward that all transactions within the scope of the system are accurately recorded and processed.
