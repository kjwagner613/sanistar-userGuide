Once logged in, you will land on the main dashboard.

System Overview

This system is designed to facilitate the following workflows:

  1. Customer master records
  2. Sales order processing
  3. Service ticket lifecycle (status, ownership, history)
  4. Live inventory with consumption
  5. SLA enforcement with risk alerts
  6. Real-time dashboards (operational state)

The system runs in PowerApps and utilizes a mix of Master Data (data that remains consistent), transactional data (data generated from day-to-day operations) to provide key indicators affecting operations.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard shows:

  • Launch Forms — Along the top, large buttons to initiate processes.
  • Individual Dashboards — Beneath the buttons are red lettered links to Dashboards
  • Date Range — Select the range for the metrics to include - Dont forget to click "Apply"

Everything updates instantly — nothing is stored or stale.

Customer Master Records

All information needed to conduct business with your clients is recorded on one of three components making up the Customer Master Record.

  • Sold-To: Information about the customer profile. Not shipping or Financial
  • Ship-To: All locastions this custoerm can recevie shipments, There can be many Ship-Tos associated with one Sold-To.
  • Bill-To: Clients prefered means of receiving invoices. Also may be many to one Sold-To.

Sales Order Processing

Sales Orders initiate downstream processes such as: build orders, thus directly affecting available parts inventory values.

  • The information entered in a Sales Order is used in every aspect of client relations including invoicing and shipping.
  • Data is pulled from the Customer Master:
  • Which is used to manage SLAs and report risk.
  • Ship-To is referenced for shipping details.
  • Bill-To data supplies A/R with the clients invoicing preferences.

Service & Build Ticket Lifecycle

Currently, there are three types of tickets:

  • Product Service
  • Product Build
  • Internal IT/Support

Each are governed by seperate SLA durations (Service & Build)

Service and Build are very similar, only Build has a product decompose step to evaluate fullfilment (MRP) and submit purschase requirements to meet demand.

Support tickets are internal are can be used to log and communicate anything that is not working, or requires attention at your site.
